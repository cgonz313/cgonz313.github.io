Craig Gonzalez is a house and techno DJ pushing the boundaries of the dance floor with carefully curated sets built on driving rhythms and deep, hypnotic grooves.

With an ear for both classic and cutting-edge sounds, his sets move seamlessly between peak-hour energy and late-night introspection — always keeping the dance floor locked in.

Available for bookings, collaborations, and residencies.
